What is DevOps? "DevOps is an increasingly common approach to software development aimed at bridging the gap between application development and IT operations. Developers and operations teams use this approach collectively to build, test, deploy, and monitor applications. It is one of the keys to making IT a relevant business partner by assuring speed, quality, and control."

Regardless of the purpose, platform, or architecture, DevOps is relevant to all kinds of software projects including cloud-native and mobile applications, application integration, modernization, and multi-cloud management to name a few. We at Meantr help you to modernize your apps with speed and control using a DevOps approach.

Why DevOps?
Whether you're modernizing applications in stages or working through a complete transformation, the benefits of DevOps can span the entire delivery pipeline. Successful DevOps implementations rely on an integrated set of solutions that help you improve deployment frequency, faster time to market, a high success rate of new releases, shortened lead time between Ô¨Åxes, and faster time to recovery.

Embracing a DevOps approach enables you to make simple processes programmable and dynamic. This in turn helps to improve operational predictability, maximize efficiency and security. By applying DevOps agile practices and tools, we at Meantr helps you reduce cost and complexity while retaining your investment in the original applications. This makes the whole project run faster and smoother by automating key steps to reduce errors, increase team agility, and to scale beyond small, isolated teams.
